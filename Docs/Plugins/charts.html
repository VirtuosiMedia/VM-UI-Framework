Charts

Chart Limitations

Charts are very flexible and dynamic, but they have some limitations. Future versions may fix these issues.

  • No animation in IE.
  • No negative numbers.
  • Data sets need to be the full-width of the graph.
  • Dates can only be on the x-axis.
  • X-values are necessary when not working with dates.
  • Updating the graph requires refreshing the canvas.
  • Large numbers may not look good on the axises.
  • Charts must be displayed at 100% of the container size.
  • Decimals?
